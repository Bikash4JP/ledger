# MobiLedger - User Guide 📖

Welcome to MobiLedger. This guide will help you set up your accounts and start tracking your finances.

## 1. Setting Up Your Language
Go to the **Settings** tab. You can toggle between **English** and **Japanese**. 
- Standard accounts like "Cash", "Sales", and "Purchases" will translate automatically.
- Custom accounts you create will remain in the language you typed.

## 2. Understanding Entry Modes
MobiLedger offers two ways to record data:
- **Cash Book**: Designed for simplicity. Use this for daily "Cash In" (Income) and "Cash Out" (Expenses).
- **Journal**: For experienced users. Use this for non-cash transactions like depreciation, credit purchases, or multi-ledger adjustments.

## 3. Managing Ledgers (Accounts)
- **Nature**: Every account must have a Nature (Asset, Liability, Income, Expense). This determines where it appears in your reports.
- **Groups**: Use groups to categorize accounts (e.g., putting "SBI Bank" and "Mizuho" under the "Bank Accounts" group).

## 4. Generating Reports
The **Reports** tab updates in real-time:
- **Trial Balance**: Checks if your total Debits equal your total Credits.
- **P&L**: Shows your net profit for the month or year.
- **Balance Sheet**: Shows your financial position (What you own vs. what you owe).

## 5. Exporting Statements
To share an account statement:
1. Open the **Ledgers** tab.
2. Select an account.
3. Filter by date if necessary.
4. Tap **Export PDF**. The file will be named `AccountName_Date_Time.pdf`.
